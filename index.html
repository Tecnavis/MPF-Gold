FAQs

Explore our frequently asked questions to learn more about gold investment options, pricing, and best practices. Feel free to reach out for personalized guidance.

How do I start investing in Gold?

Investing in gold can be done through physical purchases, ETFs, or digital platforms. Assess each option's costs, storage needs, and liquidity before making a choice.

What's the difference between coins and bars?

Gold bars are often cheaper per gram due to their larger size, while coins have higher premiums but offer more flexibility in smaller investments.

How is the price of gold determined?

Gold prices are influenced by global demand, currency values, inflation rates, and geopolitical factors.

Is CFD a good investment?

CFDs (Contracts for Difference) can be high-risk investments, offering leverage but requiring careful risk management.
